Design Principles

Berny Gutierrez

White Space & Clean Design

Company Name: LinkedIn

https://www.linkedin.com/

The LinkedIn website leaves enough white space to avoid cluttering the screen.

Alignment

Company name: Microsoft

https://www.microsoft.com/

The text, link and picture elements are all aligned to the left. Pictures and text boxes are also aligned accross the same line on the right.

Contrast

Company name: Apple

https://www.apple.com/

Contrast is used in this neat design to effectively grab attention and to make the website more redable and visually appealing. The white font on the black background combined with pictures with bright colors in black background make the text messages stand out and the pictures look elegant.

Disclosure: I'm a Microsoft employee which owns LinkedIn and is a competitor of Apple.
